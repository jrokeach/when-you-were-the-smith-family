# Contradictions

When two sources disagree — two relatives remember a holiday differently, a report card and a journal entry diverge, one parent's memory of a first word disagrees with the other — document the disagreement here rather than silently picking one.

## Why

Memory is not a source of truth. A KB that smooths over disagreement loses the texture of actual family history. When your kid reads this at 25, the fact that two grandparents remember a thing differently is itself a piece of the history.

## Format

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — short title

**Claim A:** [source] says X.
**Claim B:** [source] says Y.
**Subjects:** [kid or page](path/to/page.md), ...
**Status:** unresolved | resolved-in-favor-of-A | resolved-in-favor-of-B | left-as-both
**Notes:** context, timestamps, why resolution was chosen if any.
```
