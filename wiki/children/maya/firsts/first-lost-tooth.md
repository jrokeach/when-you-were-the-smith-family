# First Lost Tooth

Maya lost her first tooth on Valentine's Day 2023.

## Context

She was 6, almost 7. She'd been waiting for this for a long time — Leo had lost his first tooth two years earlier and she'd been jealous.

> "Leo kept bragging about it," Maya said. "I was SO ready."

## The tooth

It was her bottom left incisor. It had been loose for weeks. She'd been wiggling it constantly to the point where Sarah asked her to stop.

> "I was eating an apple and it just... POP. There it was. In my apple."

Her immediate reaction: "MOM! LOOK! I found it! I found my tooth!"

## The tooth fairy

She put it under her pillow. The tooth fairy left $5 (inflation — Leo got $2).

> "I woke up and it was GONE. And there was money. I couldn't believe it."

## What she did with it

She saved it in a little box. Then she spent the $5 on more candy.
