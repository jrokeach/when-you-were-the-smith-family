# Potty training

Fully daytime trained by 2022-04-12 after a long resistant stretch. Nighttime not consistent until 2023-ish.

## What worked

- Stickers for any attempt, not just successes.
- [Biscuit](../../../family/_examples/pets/biscuit.md) sitting next to the bathroom door somehow helped.
- Giving up on the "big kid underwear" pressure and letting Sam pick their own.

## What didn't

- Rewards too far in the future.
- Any conversation framing it as a test.
