# The shared last cookie

There was one cookie left. Sam wanted it. So did their sibling [Robin](../friends/sibling-robin.md).

Sam picked up the cookie, looked at it, broke it exactly in half, gave the bigger half to Robin, and said: *"I'm older so I can be the boss of sharing."*

No adult asked them to. No adult was in the room.

## Why I wrote this down

Because at five, Sam alternates between being the world's kindest sibling and being openly bothered that Robin exists. The kind moments I want preserved are the ones that happened when nobody was watching, because those are the ones that tell you something real.
