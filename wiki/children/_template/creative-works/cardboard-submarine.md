# Cardboard submarine

A week-long project, largely unassisted. Used:

- Two Amazon boxes
- Duct tape (gold)
- Foil for the portholes
- A flashlight taped inside, because "it's deep"
- A hand-drawn control panel with a depth gauge labeled *"0 to very"*

## Dimensions

Roughly 4 feet long. Could not leave the living room.

## Why this matters

The first project Sam stuck with for more than a single sitting. Marked the start of the long-form building interest that connects to [oceans](../interests/oceans.md) and the later [marine biologist phase](../heroes/marine-biologist-phase.md).
