# Marine biologist hero phase

Starting December 2024, Sam began referring to "marine biologists" as a category of hero. The specific inspiration: a short clip from an Octonauts episode, plus a library book about Sylvia Earle.

Sam cannot yet say "Sylvia Earle" — they say "the ocean lady." But they know she is real, and that she is 90, and they have asked twice if we can meet her.

## Preceding hero phases

- Ages 3–4: bus drivers, specifically the driver on route 74.
- Age 4–5: [Biscuit](../../../family/_examples/pets/biscuit.md). For a while Sam said "I want to be a Biscuit." Not a dog. A Biscuit.
- Age 5 (brief): the librarian at the branch, who learned Sam's name.
