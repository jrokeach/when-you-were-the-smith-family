# Sam and Biscuit

Canonical page for Biscuit the family dog: [family/pets/biscuit](../../../family/_examples/pets/biscuit.md). This page is specifically about Sam's relationship with her.

## What Biscuit means to Sam

- Biscuit was already in the house when Sam was born.
- Sam's early words included "dah" for Biscuit — see [first words](../firsts/first-words.md).
- Age 4, Sam declared they wanted to "be a Biscuit" — see [hero phases](../heroes/marine-biologist-phase.md).
- Present during most major moments: [thunderstorms](../fears/thunderstorms.md), [bedtime](../routines/bedtime-age-5.md), post-[move](../reflections/2024-post-move.md) adjustment.

## Specific moments

- 2021: Sam fed Biscuit a handful of cat food. The fallout was significant.
- 2023: Sam, at 4, sat next to Biscuit through a thunderstorm and said *"I am being brave for her."*
- 2024: Sam became aware that Biscuit is old. Started asking what happens when dogs get very old. This conversation is ongoing; see [losses/grandma-m](../losses/2024-grandma-m.md) for related processing of mortality.
