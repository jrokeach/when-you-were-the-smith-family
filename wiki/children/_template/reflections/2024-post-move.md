# How Sam is doing after the move

> _Reflections are parent observations, sometimes speculative. `confidence: medium` or `low` is often right here. These pages age well because they capture what you were noticing at the time, even if you later learned you were wrong._

Three weeks post-move, I think Sam is doing better than we expected and I don't entirely trust it.

Signs they're okay:
- Sleeping through the night consistently.
- Talking about their new classroom with specifics (not generalities).
- Still building things. [Submarine](../creative-works/cardboard-submarine.md) started this week.

Signs I'm less sure about:
- Hasn't mentioned their old best friend once. Feels like avoidance rather than acceptance.
- Slightly more clingy at drop-off, but only slightly.
- Asked twice about Grandma M — see [Grandma M](../losses/2024-grandma-m.md). I'm not sure if the move is surfacing the earlier loss or they're unrelated.

## Follow-up

Revisit in 3 months. If the "hasn't mentioned old best friend" thing persists, worth gently raising.
