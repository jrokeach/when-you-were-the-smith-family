# Dinosaurs

Theo's defining interest. He loves dinosaurs.

## How it started

Around age 3, Theo became fascinated with dinosaurs. Sarah isn't sure why — maybe a book, maybe a cartoon. It stuck.

> "They're just SO cool," Theo said. "They're like big dragons but they were REAL."

## What he knows

Theo can name — accurately — something like 30 different dinosaurs. He knows their diets, their periods, theirSizes.

- Favorite: Triceratops ("they have THREE horns")
- Second favorite: T-Rex ("obviously")
- Least favorite: Stegosaurus ("they're kind of dumb")

## What he does

- Draws dinosaurs constantly
- Has dinosaur toys everywhere
- Asks for dinosaur books for every birthday/holiday
- Dinosaur facts at dinner ("Mom, did you know...")

## His collection

He has:
- 12 dinosaur figures
- 8 dinosaur books
- 1 dinosaur pillow
- Several dinosaur shirts

## What name he'd pick

> "Theo-ro-saurus," he said, unprompted. (This is already in family vocabulary.)
