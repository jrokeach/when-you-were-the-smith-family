# First Day of Kindergarten

Theo's first day of kindergarten at Sunnyside Elementary on September 3, 2024.

## Context

He was 4 — young for kindergarten. Sarah was nervous about whether he'd be ready. He was SO ready.

> "I was SO excited," Theo said. "I wore my dinosaur shirt. My GOOD dinosaur shirt."

## The morning

He woke up at 5:30am. Couldn't go back to sleep.

Sarah made dinosaur-shaped pancakes. Theo was "too excited to eat."

## Drop-off

He walked right in. Didn't look back. Maya, in third grade, walked him to his classroom and said hi to his teacher.

> "Maya was EMBARRASSED," Theo said. "She was like 'Okay, bye' and walked away super fast."

## The day

His favorite part: "Recess." Second favorite: "Ms. Huang."

He came home with a paper dinosaur that Ms. Huang had given him. Sat and talked about it for an hour.

## How it went

First month was tough — he was tired, cranky in evenings. But by October, he was asking to go on weekends.

> "I wish school was every day," he said in November.
