# First Bike Ride

Leo learned to ride a bike without training wheels on May 15, 2017.

## The context

He was 5, almost 6. He'd been using training wheels since his birthday and was frustrated that he couldn't keep up with older kids in the neighborhood.

Sarah remembered it: "He was SO frustrated. He kept saying 'I want to be like the big kids.'"

## How he learned

David took him to the parking lot at the elementary school. Leo fell — a lot.

> "I just remember really wanting to do it," Leo said. "And then all of a sudden I didn't fall for a while and Dad was running behind me and I was like 'I'm doing it, I'm doing it!'"

## His reaction

He cried when he finally did it. Not sad — overwhelmed.

> "I did it! I didn't fall for a whole block!"

## What happened afterward

He rode up and down the street about 50 times. His legs were so tired he could barely walk the next day.

He still loves biking — it's become a family activity.
