# Driver's Permit

Leo got his learner's permit on May 1, 2026, just after his 14th birthday.

## The context

In Oregon, you can get a learner's permit at 15. But Leo turned 14 and was eager. Sarah was nervous; David was excited.

> "I've been waiting for this my whole life," Leo said. "Okay, not my whole life, but for a while."

## The test

He took the written test at the DMV. Passed on the first try — "barely," he said.

> "I was SO nervous. Everyone was looking at me. I kept thinking I'd fail and have to come back and everyone would know."

## What's happened since

He's been practicing in the family's Honda. David takes him to empty parking lots, then empty roads.

> "The first time I actually drove on a real road, my hands were shaking so bad."

He's getting more comfortable. Sarah has started letting him drive to the end of the street and back.

## What he thinks

> "It's literally the best. It's like a video game but real."

Sarah, less enthused: "Let's not get ahead of ourselves."
