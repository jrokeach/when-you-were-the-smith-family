# First Steps

Rosie took her first independent steps on April 12, 2025 — exactly one year after her birthday.

## The context

She'd been walking with help for a while — holding onto furniture, hands. But on her first birthday, she let go and took three steps.

Sarah: "We weren't even filming. We were just in the living room. David goes 'She's doing it! She's doing it!' and we scrambling for our phones."

## The steps

First steps: Three, maybe four. Then she sat down.

> "She just looked up at us like 'What?'" — Sarah

## After

She walked more the next day. By a week later, she was running.

> "The phrase 'terrible twos' makes more sense now," Sarah said. "She's into EVERYTHING."
