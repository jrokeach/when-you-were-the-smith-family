# Rosie's First Words

Rosie started talking in sentences around 15 months.

## First proper words

She said "mama" and "dada" around 10 months, but her first real word — used consistently — was "no."

Since then, she's added:

- "more" — used for food request
- "up" — arms raised
- "dog" — for Maggie
- "nana" — for Grandma Carol

## Sentences

Her first two-word sentence: "more cookie" (around 14 months).

> "She talks SO much now," Maya said. "It's like, continuous."

## The famous "Rosie-posey"

The nickname "Rosie-posey" comes from Theo's infant attempts at her name. It stuck.

> "She responds to both," Sarah said. "Rosie or Rosie-posey. She knows she's Rosalie."

## Communication style

Rosie is talkative but not always clear. She strings together sounds that seem like sentences. Adults do a lot of interpreting.

> "She's like a tiny translator," Maya said. "I have no idea what she says but she's VERY confident about it."
