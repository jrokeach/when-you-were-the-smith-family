# Powell's Books Day

The day after Thanksgiving 2024, the whole family went to Powell's City of Books in Portland.

## Context

They were in Portland for the holiday (Carol was visiting). David suggested the outing as a way to burn off turkey energy.

"It was insane," Maya said. "The store was SO big. Like, bigger than our house-big."

## What happened

- Leo found the rare books section and spent an hour there. Bought a first edition of something he won't share details about.
- Maya got lost (temporarily) in the children's section. "I wasn't lost, I was LOOKING."
- Theo sat in the middle of a stack and refused to move.
- Sarah spent $200. David spent $150. The kids combined spent $75.

## Memorable quotes

> "This is better than Disney." — Theo, age 4

> "Can we live here?" — Maya

> "I found a book from 1892." — Leo (showing off)

## Outcome

Everyone got books. Maggie got a chew toy from the gift shop (she was with them — dog-friendly day).

They went to a pizza place afterward and declared it "the best family day ever."
