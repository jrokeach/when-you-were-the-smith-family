# Maya Following Leo

For several years, Maya (4 years younger) idolized Leo and tried to emulate everything he did.

## The pattern

Maya wanted to do everything Leo did. Read what he read. Play what he played. Watch what he watched.

> "It was kind of annoying," Leo said. "She'd always want to play and I'd be like 'go away.' But also kind of cute."

Sarah noticed this dynamic and tried to encourage Leo to include her, with mixed results.

## Timeline

- **2020 (Maya 4, Leo 8)**: Most intense. Maya couldn't do anything without Leo.
- **2022 (Maya 6, Leo 10)**: Maya started developing her own interests. Still wanted Leo's approval.
- **2024 (Maya 8, Leo 12)**: Leo started middle school. They saw each other less. Maya started having her own friends.

## What changed it

Leo's shift to middle school — different schedule, different friends. Maya also started school and found her own thing.

Now they're closer as peers. Maya still admires Leo but isn't as desperate for his attention.

> "I think she's cool now," Leo said. "She has her own stuff going on."
