# Nanny Maria

Maria Delgado was the family's nanny from September 2022 until June 2024.

## Basics

- Employment: September 2022 — June 2024
- Kids: Maya (6-8), Theo (2-4), Rosie (8 months-2 years)
- Hours: Monday through Thursday, 2pm-6pm
- How found: Neighbor recommended; she had previously nannied for the Park family

## What the kids remember

> "Maria made the BEST sandwiches. Like, really thin bread. She cut the crusts off and everything. And she would do this thing where she hid raisins in the peanut butter and we'd have to find them." — Maya

> "She called me ' buddy.' I miss Maria." — Theo

## Impact

Maria was steady and calm — the kids gravitated toward her. She helped with pick-ups from school, made snacks, and managed the chaos of three kids with different schedules.

When she left in 2024 to go back to school for nursing, the whole family was sad. Maya cried. Theo didn't really understand but knew something was wrong.

"She's still text Sarah updates," Maya said recently. "She sent a picture of her baby last year."
