# Ms. Huang

Sophia Huang was Theo's kindergarten teacher at Sunnyside Elementary, 2024-2025.

## Basics

- School: Sunnyside Elementary
- Year: 2024-2025 school year
- Kids: Theo (4-5)
- Class size: 22 students

## What Theo remembers

> "She has this really big smile. One time I told her I was scared of the bathroom and she walked me there and said 'I'll guard the door' and she did."

> "She let us bring Show and Tell EVERY Friday. I brought my triceratops."

## Theo's transformation

Theo started kindergarten nervous — he'd never been at school full-day before. By December, he was asking to go on weekends.

Ms. Huang noticed his fascination with dinosaurs and incorporated it into learning. Theo "read" dinosaur books to the class during morning meeting.

"He came home SO proud," Sarah said. "That was a turning point for him."
