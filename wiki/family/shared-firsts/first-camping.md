# First Family Camping

July 2023, the Smith family's first camping trip together. Mt. Hood National Forest.

## Who went

The whole family — Sarah, David, Leo (11), Maya (7), Theo (3), and Maggie.

## Background

David had been wanting to camp since they moved to Oregon. Sarah was skeptical ("I don't sleep outside"), but the kids were excited.

They borrowed a tent from David's coworker and bought a cheap air mattress that punctured on the second night.

## What happened

- Leo assembled the tent while everyone else "supervised"
- Maya made s'mores for the first time and declared them "the best thing EVER"
- Theo was scared of the dark but loved the campfire
- Maggie was terrified of a raccoon and did not sleep

## Firsts for each kid

- Leo: First time staying up all night talking by a fire
- Maya: First s'more, first time seeing that many stars
- Theo: First time sleeping in a tent (he'd been too young before)

## Outcome

They came home dirty, tired, and wanting to do it again. Sarah declared she'd "tolerate" more camping.

They've now done it multiple times — it's become an annual tradition.
