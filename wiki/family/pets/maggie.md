# Maggie

Family golden retriever. Adopted April 2019.

## Basics

- Breed: Golden Retriever mix (rescue)
- Adopted from: PawsITIVE Rescue, Austin
- Born: ~January 2019
- Current age: ~7 years

## Origin story

The kids had been asking for a dog for years. Sarah was resistant, but David and Leo teamed up, and eventually Maya's enthusiasm won her over.

They met Maggie at a rescue event and the kids instantly claimed her. "She just picked us," Maya said. "She walked right over and sat down like 'I'm coming home with you.'"

> "I named her after my great-grandma," Leo said. "Mom said it was fine."

## What she's like

Maggie is gentle, lazy, and food-motivated. She tolerates the kids especially well, lying there while they dress her up and reading to her.

She's scared of thunder and hides in the bathroom during storms. She loves swimming.

## Relationship with each kid

- **Leo**: They used to wrestle. Now Leo ignores her, but she still sleeps at the foot of his bed.
- **Maya**: Her "person." Maya is the one who trains her and takes her on walks.
- **Theo**: Her unofficial protector. He's not gentle enough but tries.
- **Rosie**: Likes to pull her tail. Maggie tolerates this with remarkable grace.

## Fun facts

- She howls at sirens
- She's afraid of the vacuum
- Her favorite spot is the back deck in the sun
