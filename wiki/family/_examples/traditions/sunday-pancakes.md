# Sunday pancakes

Every Sunday, one parent makes pancakes; the other handles coffee and clean-up. Has been continuous since roughly 2020, with one stretch paused during the 2024 move.

## Current form

- Batter is mixed by Sam with the big wooden spoon. Non-negotiable.
- [Biscuit](../pets/biscuit.md) is given one bite of plain pancake, no syrup.
- The pancake with the weirdest shape is given a name before being eaten.

## Drift over time

- 2020: the tradition started.
- 2022: added chocolate chips after Sam campaigned for three weeks.
- 2024 (post-move): restarted after a two-month gap; Sam treated the restart like an event.

## Why write this down

In ten years when Sam doesn't want pancakes anymore, this page is all that's left.
