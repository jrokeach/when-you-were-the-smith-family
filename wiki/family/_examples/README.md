# Family reference examples

**These pages are fictional.** They demonstrate the shape of each family-category entry. They are **not this family's data** and must never be cited as facts when answering queries.

The family this scaffold was built for (unless you've populated it) has no dog named Biscuit, no Sunday pancakes tradition, no 2024 Oregon coast trip, no deceased Grandma M, and no kindergarten teacher from 2024. Those are all illustrations.

## Why they exist

Real families learn the scaffold faster when they can read well-formed examples than when they stare at empty directories with only a spec to guide them. Each example shows:

- Valid frontmatter for its page type.
- Realistic cross-links to other example pages (including the fictional child template at [`wiki/children/_template/`](../../children/_template/README.md)).
- Tone and level of detail appropriate to the category.

## What to do with them

Bootstrap offers to remove both this subtree and `wiki/children/_template/` once you've reviewed the shape. Your options:

- **Delete them.** Clean slate; your real pages will populate the parent dirs (`wiki/family/trips/`, `wiki/family/pets/`, etc.).
- **Keep them.** Lint excludes `_examples/` and `_template/` — they won't generate noise. Keep them as a living reference you or the agent can consult when uncertain about a page's shape.
