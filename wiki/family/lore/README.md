# Lore

Stories the family tells. The running jokes, the oft-retold incidents, the times something went spectacularly wrong, the big events in family history. This is the category your kid will enjoy most when they come back to this KB at 25.

## How to file

- One page per story or event.
- `YYYY-short-slug.md` if dated; just `slug.md` otherwise.
- `subjects:` includes whichever kids were involved, or `[family]` if it predates them or involves everyone.
