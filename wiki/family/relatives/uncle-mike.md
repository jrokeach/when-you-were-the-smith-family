# Uncle Mike

Michael Smith — David's younger brother. Born 1978. Lives in Seattle.

## Known to the kids

- Leo: "Uncle Mike" / "Mike" (as he's gotten older)
- Maya: "Uncle Mike"
- Theo: "Uncle Mike" / "Mikey" (his nickname for him)
- Rosie: Too young to have a clear name for him

## What he does

Mike is the fun uncle. He doesvideo calls every couple of weeks and sends the kids weird presents.

> "One time he sent us each a bug-catching kit and it was like, October," Maya said. "We were so confused."

He visits Portland 3-4 times a year, usually around holidays or random weekends. He takes each kid out individually for "special time" — a tradition he started when they were younger.

## Relationship

Mike is close to all the kids but particularly close to Leo right now. They're both into video games and tech. Mike helped Leo build his first computer.

He's less hands-on with the younger kids (not his personality) but engages them on their terms — he'll build with Theo, do art with Maya.

> "He taught me to play poker," Leo said. "Like, for pretend. He's funny."

## During Carol's illness

Mike took two months off work to help Carol during her cancer treatment in 2025. This cemented his relationship with the whole family.

"He basically saved Christmas that year," Sarah said. "We were all so stressed and he just... held it together."
