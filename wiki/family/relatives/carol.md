# Carol

Sarah's mother. Born 1955. Lives in Eugene, Oregon.

## Known to the kids

- Leo: "Grandma Carol"
- Maya: "Grandma Carol" 
- Theo: "Nana Carol"
- Rosie: "Nana"

## Background

Carol was diagnosed with breast cancer in March 2025. She completed treatment in October 2025 and is now in remission.

> "She was really brave," Leo said. "She didn't even cry. She just did what she had to do."

## Relationship with the kids

Carol is warm and effusive. She hugs too long. She sends too many gifts. The kids find this embarrassing but also endearing.

She's especially close to Maya — they'd always done "grandma-daughter time" before the diagnosis, and now they've resumed it. Maya was old enough to understand the gravity and was scared, but Carol assured her she'd be fine.

> "Nana is my favorite grandparent because she's really warm," Maya said. "Like, she actually HUGS you."

With Theo, she does "Nana things" — baking, art projects. With Rosie, she's more watchful, letting Rosie lead.

## After treatment

Carol is back to her old self, though she has less energy than before. She visits Portland quarterly now and the family goes to Eugene for school breaks.
