# Betty Smith

David's mother. Born 1952. Lives in Chicago.

## Known to the kids

- Leo: "Grandma Betty"
- Maya: "Grandma Betty"
- Theo: "B Grandma" (her nickname)
- Rosie: "Gama" (her current word for grandma)

## What she does

Betty lives in the Chicago suburb where David grew up. She visits Portland 2-3 times a year and the kids go to Chicago for Christmas every other year.

She's an avid gardener and quilter. She sends the kids handmade quilts for every birthday.

> "Grandma Betty's quilts are the BEST. She does the fabric herself and everything." — Maya

## Relationship

Betty is warm but not sentimental. She's practical and a little wry. The kids adore her because she's "funny without trying to be funny," according to Maya.

She was particularly close to Leo when he was small — they'd garden together. Now she's equally close to all of them, though Theo is her favorite right now because "he's funny."

She'd like to see the kids more often, but her arthritis makes travel harder.

## Notes

- Still drives (cautiously)
- Doesn't text much; prefers phone calls
- Never misses a birthday
