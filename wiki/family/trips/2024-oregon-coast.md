# Oregon Coast Trip

A week-long family camping trip to the Oregon coast, August 2024.

## Where they went

- Cannon Beach / Ecola State Park area
- Stayed in a cabin near Manzanita
- Drove the Coast Highway

## Who went

The whole family: Sarah, David, Leo, Maya, Theo, Rosie, and Maggie (the dog).

## Highlights

> "The beach was SO big. You could see the whole ocean." — Maya (8)

> "We found a crab! It was like this big." — Theo (4), holding hands far apart

- Leo started driving practice on empty country roads (he's learning on a learner's permit)
- Maya conquered her fear of waves
- Theo collected approximately 47 "special rocks"
- Rosie ate sand

## Low points

- It rained two days
- Maggie got sick in the car on the way home
- Theo had a meltdown over a tide pool being "ruined" when someone else's kid splashed in it

## What they remember

> "Best trip ever" — Maya (repeatedly, 2025)
> "Can we go back to the ocean?" — Theo (asked about 30 times since)
