# Rosie Joins the Family

Rosalind (Rosie) Smith was born on April 12, 2024.

## The pregnancy

Sarah's pregnancy was straightforward but exhausting. Maya was 8 and understood she was "having a baby in her tummy." Theo, at 4, didn't really get it until they brought her home.

> "I wanted a baby sister," Maya said. "I got a baby sister. IT WORKED."

## Birth

Rosie was born at Oregon Health & Science University. David was there, along with Grandma Betty (who flew in for the occasion).

Leo was 12 and stayed with Uncle Mike. Maya was at a friend's house. Theo was with neighbors.

> "I came to the hospital and she was SO small," Maya said. "Tiny. I was scared I'd break her."

## Meeting her

- Leo: "She's kind of ugly when she's born. Then she got cute."
- Maya: "I cried. Just a little."
- Theo: "Baby?"

## The adjustment

The transition was mercifully easy. Rosie was a good baby. The older kids — especially Maya — doted on her.

Maya wanted to hold her constantly. Leo pretended not to care but would quietly sit by her crib.

Theo, at 4, was jealous at first ("That's MY baby") but quickly adjusted.

## Name

- "Rosie" came from the fact that she was born with rosy cheeks.
- "Rosie-posey" was what Theo called her as an infant and it stuck.
