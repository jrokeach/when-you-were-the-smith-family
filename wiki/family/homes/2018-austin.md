# Austin Years

The Smith family's first house after moving from Chicago, 2018-2022.

## The context

They moved to Austin when Leo was finishing first grade. David's new job opportunity meant leaving Chicago — where Sarah's parents (Betty and Frank) lived.

"We knew it was permanent this time," Sarah said. "In Chicago we'd always felt like we were between things. In Austin, we made it home."

## The house

A 1920s bungalow in Hyde Park, three bedrooms, one bathroom, a big kitchen. It had a front porch that became the family's spot in evenings.

"The porch was where everything happened," David said. "Homework, popsicles, Just hanging out. The kids would fight over who sat in the blue chair."

## Memories

- Leo started atPeabody Elementary in first grade. His teacher was Ms. Gonzalez, who he remembered dearly.
- Maya was in preschool at Trinity Presbyterian. She remembered playing in the "mud kitchen" outside.
- The family got Maggie (the dog) in 2019 — a golden retriever puppy from a rescue.
- Leo lost his first tooth at Grandma Betty's house in Chicago during Christmas 2019.

## Why they left

The house was small for a growing family and no longer had room for a home office. Also, summer heat was getting harder to manage with little kids.
