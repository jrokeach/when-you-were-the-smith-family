# Portland Move

The Smith family moved from Austin, Texas to Portland, Oregon in July 2022.

## The decision

Sarah and David had been talking about a change for a couple of years. Leo was about to start middle school, and they wanted a community with more walkability and nature access. When David's company went fully remote, the timing felt right.

"We talked to the kids about it ahead of time," Sarah said. "Leo was excited. Maya was sad to leave her friends. Theo didn't really understand. Rosie was only eight months old."

## The move

Leo remembered helping pack his own books into boxes. Maya cried when her best friend Jayden came over to say goodbye.

The drive took three days — they stopped in Memphis to see David's college roommate.

## Adjusting to Portland

> "The first thing Theo said when we pulled into the driveway was, 'Where are the trees?' And then a week later he was like, 'Can we live here forever?'" — Sarah

The house had a big backyard, which thrilled Theo. Maya missed her friends but quickly made new ones at Sunnyside Elementary. Leo started sixth grade at West Portland Middle School and was nervous about switching schools mid-childhood.

 Rosie, nearly 2, adapted quickly — she loved having space to run around and soon discovered the backyard sandbox.

## What they remember

- Leo: "I was worried about starting middle school in a new place, but it turned out fine."
- Maya: "I missed Jayden a lot. But I made friends with Stella and we still video call."
- Theo: "The backyard is the best. We have a HUGE yard."
